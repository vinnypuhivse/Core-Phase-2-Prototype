Student Learning Objectives

Content Objective

Students will analyze how Ponyboy's point of view reveals social divides that shape his identity and sense of belonging.

Language Objective

Students will use transition words (because, as a result, therefore) to explain connections between point of view and empathy.

Foundational Skill Focus

Morphology and fluency with complex sentences from the text.

Essential Question

What does it take to learn something difficult?
