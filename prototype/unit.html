Unit Context
🌱 Knowledge-Building Topic

Belonging and Social Constructs - Youth Identity, Class, and Peer Belonging in America

💡 I'm Learning About
  • How friendships, loyalty, and conflict shape identity.
  • How class differences affect belonging and choices.
  • How literature builds empathy by showing multiple perspectives.
🎯 Knowledge Goals
  • Analyze how friendships and loyalties influence decision-making.
  • Evaluate how point of view builds empathy across divides.
  • Compare how different social groups experience belonging.
❓ Knowledge-Building Questions
  • How do social groups shape individual identity?
  • What forces push people together or apart across social divides?
  • How does our sense of belonging change as we grow?
🔑 Essential Questions
  • What makes a friendship last through change?
  • Why do people react to the same situation in differently?
  • SEL: perspective-taking, identity, community awareness.
